## Tips for Writing Your Content

### Bio Section
- **Be personal**: Include personality traits, values, communication style
- **Be specific**: Instead of "I like technology," say "I'm fascinated by how AI can solve real-world problems"
- **Show character**: Let your unique voice come through
- **Include context**: Where you grew up, what influences you, your journey

### Resume Section
- **Be detailed**: More information = better AI responses
- **Use action verbs**: Led, Built, Improved, Managed, etc.
- **Include metrics**: Numbers help quantify your impact
- **Add context**: Why you made career moves, what you learned
- **Be honest**: Only include real experiences

### What to Include

✅ **Do Include:**
- Specific projects and technologies
- Quantifiable achievements
- Your communication style and personality
- Interests and hobbies
- Career transitions and reasons
- Learning experiences
- Failures and lessons learned
- Current goals and aspirations
- What excites you professionally
- How you work with teams

❌ **Don't Include:**
- Sensitive personal information
- Private details you don't want to share
- Confidential company information
- Anything you wouldn't put on a public resume

## How to Use This Template

1. Create a new Google Doc
2. Copy the template above
3. Fill in all sections with your information
4. Add as much detail as you're comfortable sharing
5. Remember: More context = better AI responses
6. Save the document
7. Copy the document ID from the URL
8. Add it to your `.env` file as `GOOGLE_DOC_ID`

## Updating Your Information

The backend automatically fetches your Google Doc every 5 minutes, so you can:
- Edit your doc anytime
- Add new experiences
- Update your skills
- The AI will automatically use the latest information

**Pro Tip**: The more conversational and detailed your bio, the more natural your digital twin will sound. Don't just list facts - tell your story!
